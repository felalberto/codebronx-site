What We Do

CodeBronx helps schools strengthen computational thinking and computing education through practical integration, instructional support, and coherent programming that can be sustained over time.

Computational Thinking Integration

We support educators in embedding computational thinking into subjects like ELA, math, and other content areas so students can build problem solving, reasoning, and structured thinking through everyday instruction.

Computing Education Program Guidance

We help schools add, strengthen, or modify current computing education and technology programming so it is more intentional, instructionally strong, and aligned to broader learning goals.

Project Curation Across Subjects

We curate and design interdisciplinary projects that connect computing, school themes, and academic content, helping students apply computing concepts in meaningful and connected ways.

Proof, Not Hype

CodeBronx focuses on instructional impact, meaningful integration, and stronger computing education experiences for students and teachers.
